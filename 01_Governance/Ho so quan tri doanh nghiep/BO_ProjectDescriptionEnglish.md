# PART I: COMPASS VITALS GOVERNANCE
### I. CORPORATE PHILOSOPHY & STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK
### 1. Mission
-
To become a provider of convenient – comprehensive - affordable online healthcare
services through the use of new technologies.
### • Convenience: No language barriers, faster access, no travel or waiting time
required, accessible at a convenient time, easy to use, and clear information.
### • Comprehensive: Services are designed to fully support customers under the
### holistic healthcare concept, delivering complete information and services.
### • Affordable: Operational costs are maintained at 1/10–1/20 of traditional models,
allowing service fees to remain at the lowest possible level.
### 2. Vision
-
Within the first two years, to become the leading online healthcare service provider for
the Vietnamese community in the United States, with a minimum of 5,000 active
customers. In the following two years, to expand to other communities.
### 3. Core Values
-
An outsourced operational model combining teams in Vietnam and the United States,
utilizing AI to increase efficiency and reduce costs.
-
Specialized AI agents supporting professional tasks and operations.
-
A holistic approach to healthcare: Performance Medicine – Primary Care Medicine–
Lifestyle Medicine – Evidence-Based Medicine.
-
A dedicated workforce aligned with shared goals and committed to continuous
improvement
### 4. Stakeholders
-
### Early-stage investors: Compass Vitals is committed to achieving efficiency to ensure
that early investors gain benefits while actively contributing to the development of the
Compass Vitals system.
-
Workforce: Ensure opportunities for professional development and maintain a
company culture built on Equality – Caring – Empathy.
-
Customers: Continuously seek solutions that enable customers to access online
healthcare services that are Convenient – Comprehensive - Affordable.
