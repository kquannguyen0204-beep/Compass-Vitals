01.
Introduction

Who We Are

Compass Vitals is a fundamentally new healthcare model built specifically for Vietnamese
Americans.  We  are  not  another  telehealth  platform—we  represent  a  new  paradigm
combining bilingual physicians, intelligent technology, and deep cultural understanding to
deliver comprehensive, convenient, and affordable care.

Our hybrid model unites physicians in Vietnam and Vietnamese-speaking doctors in the
U.S.  as  a  unified  care  team,  empowered  by  advanced  technology.  We  provide  24/7
continuous care, seamlessly coordinated from consultation to lab testing and specialist
referrals—all in both Vietnamese and English.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

OUR MISSION - To provide convenient, comprehensive, and affordable online healthcare
by leveraging new technology to eliminate language, time, and distance barriers, ensuring
continuous care for customers at a cost-effective rate.

OUR  VISION  -  To  become  the  leading  trusted  online  healthcare  provider  for  the
Vietnamese community in the U.S. within the first two years, by perfecting the Vietnam-U.S.
hybrid care model and delivering core services with superior and consistent quality.

CORE  VALUE  -  We  don’t  just  translate—we  understand.  Compass  Vitals  utilizes
advanced  technology  to  optimize  workflows  and  reduce  costs  without  ever  losing  the
human touch. Every technological solution is reinforced by a dual-layer physician review
process to ensure absolute accuracy and safety.

02.
Logo

Logo & Meaning

The Identity of Excellence Our brand identity is more than a visual guide; it is a promise
of consistency and excellence. The Compass logo integrates the medical cross with a
navigational  compass,  signaling  our  commitment  to  guiding  patients  toward  holistic,
360-degree health. Rooted in the word "Compassion," our name reflects a dedication
to thoughtful, all-encompassing care.
